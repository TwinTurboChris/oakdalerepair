Auto Repair and Maintenance

Automotive Repair Services

At Oakdale Auto Repair, we offer a full range of automotive repair and maintenance services to keep your vehicle safe, reliable, and performing at its best. Our experienced ASE-certified technicians use state-of-the-art equipment and high-quality parts to ensure every repair is done right the first time.

Tires and Brakes

Getting new tires is one of the best ways to keep your vehicle running safely and smoothly. At Oakdale Auto Repair, we offer a wide selection of tires at competitive prices to fit your vehicle and budget.

Our factory-trained and ASE-certified brake experts specialize in complete front and rear brake services. We use premium-quality brake pads, shoes, rotors, and drums to ensure optimal stopping power and safety.

Steering and Suspension

A well-maintained steering and suspension system is essential for a smooth and safe ride. As vehicles stay on the road longer, regular maintenance becomes even more important. Oakdale Auto Repair recommends a complete steering and suspension inspection at least twice a year or whenever you notice unusual tire wear, pulling, or excessive bouncing.

Electrical and Diagnostic Repair

Today’s vehicles are more complex than ever, with advanced electrical and computer-controlled systems. Oakdale Auto Repair uses the latest diagnostic equipment to accurately identify and repair electrical or drivability concerns. Our ASE-certified technicians will find and fix the problem quickly and efficiently.

Lube, Oil, and Filter Service

Regular oil changes are critical to your engine’s longevity and performance. We provide oil and filter changes for all makes and models, including luxury cars, high-performance vehicles, and heavy-duty diesel trucks.

Factory Scheduled Maintenance

Following your vehicle manufacturer’s recommended maintenance schedule is the best way to extend the life of your car and avoid costly repairs. At Oakdale Auto Repair, our ASE-certified technicians can perform all factory-scheduled maintenance at a fraction of the dealership cost—without voiding your manufacturer’s warranty.

Air Conditioning and Heating Systems

Don’t get caught in extreme temperatures with a failing A/C or heater. Our technicians at Oakdale Auto Repair are certified A/C and heating specialists. We provide complete system diagnostics, refrigerant recharges, leak detection, and repairs to keep your climate control system running efficiently year-round.

And Much More…

From engine repairs and transmission services to battery replacements and alignments, Oakdale Auto Repair is your one-stop shop for all your automotive needs. Contact us today with any questions or to schedule your next service appointment!
